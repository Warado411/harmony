Contrast Harmony

Emotional Manifesto

My work is born from personal observations of other peoples, cultures, nature and its surroundings.

I process my experiences, combining them with imagination and emotional state, while intuition gives me the courage to experiment and freedom.

One of the most recognizable images is the symbiosis of a panther and a leopard, symbolizing the relationship with my sister, with whom we founded the artistic alliance cheltsoviii, where different ceramic objects are presented.

Contrast harmony for me is a combination of opposite elements of different colors and active forms.
